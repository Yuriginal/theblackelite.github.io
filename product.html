Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the best way to use a Black Vape pod?

Black Vape pods are designed to operate under regular inhalation pressure. Excessive forced inhalation may push fluid into the airflow system. It’s important to refrain from biting or pressing the mouthpiece too hard with your lips, as this could also apply undue pressure, leading to fluid entering the air stream and potentially causing a leak.

Can I take the Black Vape device and/or Black Vape pod on an airplane?

Black Vape equipment is safe for transport, however, numerous airlines place limitations on the usage of vaporizers within the plane. Additionally, many airlines institute restrictions related to devices possessing lithium batteries. For specific rules and constraints, it’s recommended to reach out to your chosen airline or refer to their provided list of items that are not permitted.
